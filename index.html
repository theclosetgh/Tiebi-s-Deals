FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Still unsure? WhatsApp us directly.

How long does data delivery take?

Data is typically delivered within 15–30 minutes of payment. This depends on the network load at the time of your order. You can track your order status live on screen after paying.

Can I buy data for someone else's number?

Yes! You can top up any phone number on any supported network — yours, a family member's, or a friend's. Just enter their number at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN Mobile Money, AirtelTigo Money, Telecel Cash, and debit/credit cards. All payments are processed securely via Paystack.

What if I entered the wrong number?

Please double-check the number before paying. If you made an error, contact us immediately on WhatsApp at 0503674353 and we will do our best to help you.

Why are your prices cheaper than the network directly?

We buy data in bulk at wholesale rates and pass the savings on to you. This is completely legal and the bundles are 100% genuine network data.

Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. We do not store any card or MoMo details. All transactions are handled by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant processor trusted across Africa.

How do I reach customer support?

The fastest way is WhatsApp — message us at 0503674353. We're available 7 days a week and typically respond within minutes.
