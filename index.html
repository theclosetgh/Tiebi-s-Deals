FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We have answers. Still unsure? WhatsApp us directly.

How long does data delivery take?

Data is typically delivered within 15-30 minutes of payment. You can track your order status live on screen after paying.

Can I buy data for someone else's number?

Yes! You can top up any phone number on any supported network - yours, a family member's, or a friend's.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN Mobile Money, AirtelTigo Money, Telecel Cash, and debit/credit cards via Paystack.

What if I entered the wrong number?

Contact us immediately on WhatsApp at 0503674353 and we will do our best to help you.

Why are your prices cheaper than the network directly?

We buy data in bulk at wholesale rates and pass the savings on to you. 100% genuine network data.

Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. All transactions are handled by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant processor trusted across Africa.

How do I reach customer support?

WhatsApp us at 0503674353. We are available 7 days a week and respond within minutes.
